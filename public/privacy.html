Privacy Policy

WhatsApp CRM Chrome Extension — Last updated: June 2026

Summary

This extension runs locally in your browser. Lead data, reminders, campaigns, and settings are stored in IndexedDB on your device via Chrome storage APIs. We do not operate a central server that collects your WhatsApp messages.

Data we process

  • Contact names, phone numbers, and chat metadata you save as leads
  • Reminder and campaign content you create
  • WhatsApp message text read from the active chat tab when you use capture, bulk send, or AI auto-reply

Data storage

All CRM data stays on your computer unless you enable optional CRM webhook sync in Settings.

Third-party services

  • WhatsApp Web — messaging happens through your logged-in session
  • Ollama (optional) — if enabled, message context is sent to your local Ollama instance for AI replies
  • Your webhook URL (optional) — lead sync sends data you configure

Permissions

  • storage, alarms, notifications, sidePanel, tabs — extension features
  • web.whatsapp.com — read/send messages in your WhatsApp tab
  • localhost:11434 — optional local AI (Ollama)

WhatsApp terms

Automated messaging may violate WhatsApp's Terms of Service. Use bulk and auto-reply features responsibly and only with contacts who have opted in.
