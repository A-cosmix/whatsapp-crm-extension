Privacy Policy

Explain Like WhatsApp — Last updated: June 2026

What We Collect

  • Account data: Email address and display name when you sign up
  • Usage data: Explanation history, preferred modes, streak data, achievements
  • Selected text: Text you highlight is sent to our AI provider for explanation
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage metrics (modes used, explanation counts)

What We Don't Collect

  • We do not read your full browsing history
  • We do not access passwords or financial data on websites
  • We do not sell your personal data

Data Storage

API keys (if you use Claude) are stored locally in Chrome storage on your device. User profiles are stored in Firebase Firestore. Cached explanations are stored in IndexedDB and auto-deleted after 30 days.

Third-Party Services

  • CosmiQ AI (Hugging Face): Default AI — selected text is sent for explanation
  • Claude API (Anthropic): Optional paid AI — only if you add your own API key
  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and user data storage
  • Razorpay: Payment processing for Pro subscriptions

Your Rights

You can export or delete your data anytime from Settings. You can delete your account from Settings → Delete Account.
